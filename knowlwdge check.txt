What is the difference between margin and padding in CSS?
Margin controls the space outside the element, creating separation between the element and its 
neighboring elements.
Padding controls the space between the element's content and its border.

Difference between position relative and absolute?

position: relative;:
When an element is positioned as relative, it remains in the normal flow of the document.

position: absolute;:
When an element is positioned as absolute, it is taken out of the normal flow of the document.
